Why Compare?

Choosing the right browser automation tool depends on your use case:

  • E2E Testing - Playwright or Selenium work well for testing your own apps
  • Web Scraping - Airbrowser's anti-detection is essential for protected sites
  • AI Agents - Airbrowser's MCP integration is purpose-built for LLM access
  • Scale - Airbrowser manages 100+ browsers with built-in pooling
